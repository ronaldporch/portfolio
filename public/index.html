About Me


I'm just a developer who likes to solve any problem that's in his way. If there's a way to do it in code, then it's going to be tested, solved, refactored, and solved again.

I gained most of my passion and knowledge of software development from Code Fellows, a coding bootcamp in the lovely city of Seattle. I graduated with a certificate in Ruby on Rails which a heavy focus on Javascript frameworks. I think I fell in love with AngularJS then. Ever since, I've been learning as much as I can about all the cool buzzwordy frameworks that are all aiming towards one goal: a modern web ecosystem that is responsive and can communicate across platforms, mobile, desktop, tablet and more.

Since graduating, I've been spending my time sharpening my toolset along with developing new skills, like iOS/Swift App development. But when I'm not doing that, I love to read, study Japanese, and being near water.

I hope to hear from you soon.
